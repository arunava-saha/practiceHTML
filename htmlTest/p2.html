Indian Institute of Technology

The Indian Institutes of Technology (IT) are autonomous public institutes of higher education, located in India. They are governed by the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961 which has declared them as Institutions of national Importance alongside National Institutes of Technology and lays down their powers, duties, and framework for governance etc.
